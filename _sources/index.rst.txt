sms2jwplayer: export SMS content to jwplayer
============================================

The ``sms2jwplayer`` implements various pieces useful for the export of data
from the `existing University streaming media service <https://sms.cam.ac.uk/>`_
to the `jwplayer platform <https://developer.jwplayer.com/jw-platform/>`_.
